## **CASCO Briefing: “A New Species of Asexual, Asocial Males” – CNN / Scott Galloway (Nov 2025)**

### **1. Artifact Overview**

* **Source:** CNN Interview (Anderson Cooper 360 segment; circulating as YouTube Short [https://youtube.com/shorts/ghEasXcwZcY](https://youtube.com/shorts/ghEasXcwZcY))
* **Speaker:** Prof. Scott Galloway (NYU Stern)
* **Date:** ≈ October 31 – November 1, 2025
* **Narrative frame:** Economic and technological systems have inadvertently engineered a generation of socially withdrawn young men, undermining social cohesion and reproduction.

---

### **2. Core Claims / Propositions**

1. **Behavioral data point:** ~45 % of men aged 18-25 have never approached a woman in person.
2. **Causal chain:**

   * Global economy consolidated under ~10 “Magnetism 10” tech giants.
   * These firms profit from “enragement & engagement loops.”
   * Algorithms sequester youth—especially males—into virtual or AI-mediated life.
3. **Moral thesis:** We are “evolving a new species of asocial, asexual males,” amounting to a self-extinction trend driven by economic incentives.
